# PM Competitive Analysis

Use this skill for competitor teardown, competitive matrices, feature comparisons, positioning, differentiation opportunities, and market landscape analysis.

## Workflow

1. Clarify category, target customers, competitors, evaluation criteria, and the product decision this analysis supports.
2. Separate observable facts from inferred strategy.
3. Compare user jobs, workflows, pricing/packaging signals, onboarding, feature depth, integrations, trust, and switching costs.
4. Identify parity gaps, differentiation opportunities, and traps to avoid.
5. End with product implications, not just a matrix.

## Output

- 分析目标与竞品范围
- 竞品矩阵
- 功能/流程拆解
- 差异化机会
- 用户与商业风险
- 可验证假设
- 对产品路线图的建议

## Guardrails

- Browse or ask for current source material when up-to-date claims matter.
- Do not copy competitor wording as product strategy.
- Mark unverifiable claims as assumptions.
