# PM Discovery

Use this skill for user research plans, interview scripts, discovery questions, interview analysis, pain point synthesis, opportunity mapping, and jobs-to-be-done work.

## Workflow

1. Clarify the research objective, target segment, decision to inform, timeline, and known hypotheses.
2. Design non-leading research questions focused on behavior, context, alternatives, costs, and triggers.
3. For interview analysis, extract evidence-backed themes, not generic opinions.
4. Group findings by user segment, frequency, severity, and business relevance.
5. End with opportunity areas and what evidence is still missing.

## Output

- 研究目标与关键问题
- 招募对象与样本建议
- 访谈提纲或调研任务
- 分析框架：痛点、触发场景、现有替代方案、影响、证据
- 机会点列表与优先级
- 风险、偏差和待验证假设
- 下一步研究或实验建议

## Guardrails

- Avoid asking users to design the solution.
- Do not treat a single quote as a validated pattern.
- Mark weak evidence and sampling bias clearly.
