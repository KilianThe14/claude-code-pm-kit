# PM Meeting Synthesis

Use this skill for meeting notes, decision logs, action-item extraction, stakeholder recaps, and risk/status summaries.

## Workflow

1. Identify meeting purpose, attendees, decisions, open questions, disagreements, and promised follow-ups.
2. Preserve uncertainty: if an owner, date, or decision is missing, mark it as missing instead of guessing.
3. Consolidate repetitive discussion into concise decisions and rationale.
4. Separate actions from discussion notes.
5. Produce a version that can be pasted into a team channel.

## Output

- 会议结论
- 已确认决策
- 行动项：owner、动作、截止时间
- 风险与阻塞
- 待确认问题
- 背景摘要
- 可发送版本

## Guardrails

- Do not invent decisions from unresolved debate.
- Keep sensitive or personal comments out of broad stakeholder summaries unless necessary.
- Flag contradictions in the source notes.
