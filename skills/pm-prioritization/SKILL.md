# PM Prioritization

Use this skill when the user needs to rank features, decide roadmap order, compare opportunities, or justify trade-offs.

## Workflow

1. Confirm the decision horizon, team capacity, strategic goals, must-do commitments, and constraints.
2. Choose the simplest useful framework:
   - RICE for roadmap trade-offs.
   - ICE for early ideas with weak data.
   - MoSCoW for release scope.
   - Kano for satisfaction and delight trade-offs.
3. Build a score table with confidence and evidence quality.
4. Call out sensitivity: what changes the ranking if estimates are wrong.
5. Recommend an ordered plan with clear rationale and risks.

## Output

- 评分维度与口径
- 排序表
- Top bets、quick wins、defer/drop
- 关键取舍理由
- 依赖和容量风险
- 需要补充的数据
- 建议路线图或下一步

## Guardrails

- Do not hide weak inputs behind precise-looking scores.
- Treat compliance, security, contractual commitments, and severe user-impacting defects as constraints before scoring.
- Explain why lower-ranked items are deferred.
