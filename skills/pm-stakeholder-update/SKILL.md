# PM Stakeholder Update

Use this skill when the user needs status updates, decision memos, executive summaries, team announcements, launch updates, or audience-specific product communications.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, desired decision or reaction, status, timeline, risks, and asks.
2. Tailor emphasis:
   - Executives: outcome, risk, decision, business impact.
   - Engineering: scope, dependencies, trade-offs, timeline.
   - Design: user problem, flows, research signals.
   - Sales/CS: customer value, positioning, objections, enablement.
   - Support/Ops: operational impact, known issues, escalation path.
3. Keep the update short, scannable, and explicit about asks.

## Output

- 一句话状态
- 本周进展
- 关键决策或变化
- 风险与阻塞
- 需要对方做什么
- 下一步时间线
- 按受众改写版本

## Guardrails

- Do not bury asks at the end.
- Avoid overclaiming progress when dependencies are unresolved.
- Use plain language, not internal shorthand unless provided by the user.
