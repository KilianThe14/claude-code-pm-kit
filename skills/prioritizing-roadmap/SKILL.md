# Prioritizing Roadmap

Use this skill when the user needs roadmap trade-offs, sequencing, quarterly planning, initiative ranking, or a rationale for what to do now, later, or not at all.

Source note: inspired by RefoundAI Lenny Skills `prioritizing-roadmap` (MIT). This version is written for this kit.

## Workflow

1. Define planning horizon, strategic goals, capacity, committed work, and decision constraints.
2. Normalize roadmap candidates into comparable initiatives with problem, user, outcome, evidence, and rough effort.
3. Separate must-do obligations from discretionary bets.
4. Score using a pragmatic roadmap lens:
   - Strategic fit.
   - User/customer impact.
   - Business impact.
   - Evidence confidence.
   - Effort and dependency load.
   - Time sensitivity.
   - Risk and reversibility.
5. Sequence by dependency, learning value, launch window, and team capacity.
6. Produce a communication-ready roadmap narrative, including what is deliberately deferred.

## Output

- Roadmap context
- Candidate normalization table
- Priority scorecard
- Now/Next/Later recommendation
- Sequencing rationale
- Capacity and dependency risks
- Deferred items and rationale
- Stakeholder narrative

## Guardrails

- Do not let loud stakeholders outrank validated user/business impact without saying so.
- Do not score incomparable items until they are normalized to outcomes.
- Include trade-offs; a roadmap that says yes to everything is not a roadmap.
