# Stakeholder Alignment

Use this skill when the user needs buy-in, cross-functional alignment, executive approval, stakeholder mapping, objection handling, or a decision meeting plan.

Source note: inspired by RefoundAI Lenny Skills `stakeholder-alignment` (MIT). This version is written for this kit.

## Workflow

1. Identify the decision, stakeholders, influence, concerns, incentives, and required level of support.
2. Map stakeholders:
   - Decider.
   - Strong supporters.
   - Skeptics.
   - Implementers.
   - Consulted experts.
   - Informed groups.
3. Clarify what each stakeholder needs: evidence, risk mitigation, timeline, customer impact, resourcing, or ownership.
4. Prepare the alignment narrative: problem, evidence, options, trade-offs, recommendation, ask.
5. Plan pre-wires, decision meeting, and post-meeting follow-through.
6. Track objections as product inputs or decision risks, not personal resistance.

## Output

- Stakeholder map
- Alignment objective
- Objection and concern table
- Pre-wire plan
- Decision memo
- Meeting agenda
- Follow-up actions
- Escalation path

## Guardrails

- Do not confuse informing stakeholders with aligning them.
- Make the ask explicit: approve, unblock, resource, decide, review, or change behavior.
- Preserve trade-offs; over-smoothing risks creates late-stage disagreement.
